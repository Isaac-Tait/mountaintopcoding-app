Teaching myself web development one keystroke at a time


Over the last three or four years I have built three websites Fallfish Tenkara, Cutthroat Tenkara, & Macadam Grinding using the venerable content management system - Wordpress. Through that process of building those sites I have come to be curious how Wordpress, and all of its millions of plugins, actually work. As a stay at home dad I would like to be able to learn a skill that I can use to "make some bread" without having to leave home. So, using Digital Ocean and Codecademy I have jumped in head first, so to speak, into the coding world.

Thank you for visiting and stay tuned as I bring my newfound skills into a tangible medium


My Roadmap

  1. Learn HTML5 - I first learned HTML2 from a friends grandpa who taught a homeschoolers curriculum. His granddaughter, Takara, was my first real crush. My original motivation for attending his class was in the hopes of catching a glimpse of Takara. Problem was I was too scared to look at her...
  2. Learn CSS - At first I was very intimidated by CSS. Just looking at it I had NO idea what the heck was going on. However, in just a few short days of using Codecademy my understanding and comfort with CSS has grown exponentially.
  3. Launch this website - I know I need to do something about my ever growing collection of Digital Ocean Droplets and URL's. Perhaps I will create a web developers anonymous group...
  4. Learn JavaScript - I tried once before using a book I purchased at Barnes & Noble. The book was thicker than my Bible and WAY more dry. Let us hope this time around the learning process goes a little better...
  5. Fine tune my web development skills with a lot of practice. This is the fun part. I have created a One Journal to keep track of all the code I am learning.
